The Algorithm
How CineMatch works
01
Feature extraction
Each movie is converted into a feature vector using its genres (weight 2×) and keywords (weight 1×), forming a rich representation in high-dimensional space.
02
Preference modelling
Your selected movies are merged into a single user preference vector by summing their feature vectors — capturing the essence of your taste.
03
Cosine similarity
We compute similarity(A,B) = (A·B)/(|A|×|B|) between your vector and every candidate film. Values near 1 = near-perfect match.
04
Ranked results
Movies are sorted by score, filtered to exclude your picks, and the top 8 are surfaced with match percentages and explanatory tags.
